Hamburger Recipe

Back to Home Page

Need a basic burger recipe that you can whip up in no time? We have the perfect one. With just 5 ingredients and 15 minutes of cooking time, this flavorful recipe is ready to be shared, topped, and sauced in any way you like!

Nothing says summer quite like a juicy grilled burger. You'll find the full ingredient list below with step-by-step instructions.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup fine dry bread crumbs

Steps:

  1. Preheat grill
  2. Whisk egg, salt, and pepper together in bowl
  3. Add ground beef and bread crumbs to mixture
  4. Divide into four patties
  5. Place patties on grill, cooking 6 - 8 minutes per side.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
